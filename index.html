Welcome to G-Coin Bot

The G-Coin Bot is a unique Discord bot that rewards community members with G-Coins based on their engagement on social media. Users earn rewards for liking and commenting on Instagram posts.

Why Do We Need Instagram API Access?

To automate the process of tracking user engagement, we require access to the Instagram API. This allows us to retrieve a list of users who have liked and commented on our posts, ensuring they receive the appropriate rewards within our Discord community.
